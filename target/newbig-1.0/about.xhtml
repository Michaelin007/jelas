About

NutriSoft is a private web application own by Olayemi Michael a clinical nutritionist and a java developer,he hold a degree in nutrition and dietetics from the ivory tower Federal University of Agriculture Abeokuta,he designed this web application to help folks out there live healthy lifestyle. This platform provide a connection between a clinical nutritionists/ Dietitians and people out there who wish to improve their well being through nutrition. Every questions asked will be responded to with 48 hours I and other experts of group will will respond to your questions. Our response will be directly sent to you e-mail.

This platform will also celebrate experts in the field of Nutrition and Dietetics by listing them on our top 10 nutrition professionals of the month. To get this slot, they must provide answer to our monthly critical question and this could improve their private practice.

I really love nutrition and will be happy to help others achieve healthy lifestyle through Nutrition
